About

Exirt is the solo project of producer & songwriter Nikita Dymchuk who was born in Poltava, Ukraine.
Bet,T is the first released single by Exirt. This song is the result of unrequited love that he went through. With the feeling of emptiness he started to rethink all that happend and did what he loved the most – played guitar and searched for the cure in the songs of his favourite artists.
Bet,T is a heartbreaking love story that could be familiar to anyone.
